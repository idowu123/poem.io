Can we call it madness
When democracy turn to crazyness

I wish you are here Author
To see it yourself

Then

This is madness

If it is not crazyness
Why do we have to travel
Millions if miles
To iron our cloth and get our phone charge

If it is not madness Why do will have to trek from one village to get kerosine and pectroleum

And continue suffering in the hands
Of the present Gladiator

When they sake the power of thination

Even many ghost woke to partook in their manifestoes
Promising to abide by the constitution of the land

Now
Constitution as their attribution
The roof of this nation is leaking
Our landlords are there doing nothing
Nothing but for nothing

This nation
Call for repainting
Call for rebranding
Tell the corrupt politician
To be aware of the comming sinister
Hope we will not suffer like this
In the hand of the next Gladiator.
