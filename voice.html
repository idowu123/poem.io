Voice of Chidren
By Solomon Idowu Michael



We are here again!!!
The children of the street apartheid
We are, that children
Whose slept under the
Subway path and bridges
We are the one hustling
In the street of Lagos
We are here ramble the whole of Abuja
We are the one you call slave
We are the one whom
You pray for everyday
We are the one who is in need
Of wardrobe allowance

We are the son of that tax collector
Whom you accused of
Stealing your money
We are the clan of whom you never
Hear his name before
We are the son of that
Sole mender
Hunter
Farmer
Sweeper
Beggar
Archer
Maid
Slave
Whom you maltreat and cause
And deride in the street everyday

We are the children who
Have no place to sleep
We are children who lack three square meal a day
We are not born with silver spoon
We are the children of
That poor old grey
Hair woman in old 33 church yard Road
We are the one whom
You denial of bone
From your fatty meat

We are children that doesnt fear tiger
We are children chanting your name
Whenever you pass
But you do spread dust on our eye
With the first locomotive
Speed of your car
We are children screaming your name
We wonder why you never
Heard our voices
When will you listen to us?
When will you stop fighting?
Over sit in house of assemble
And listen to our grumble and rumble

When will you remember us?
We are children whom
Father work in your ban
We are that little children
With bright future
When are we going to enjoy
That fat of our Father land
Whom serve his fore father land?
In pain and patriot

Stop fighting over sit
And remember the poor children
We are the future of this our father fore-father land.
We are tomorrow leader
