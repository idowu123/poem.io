The day we call you
To this earth
We heard the rainbow scream
Across the face of beautiful earth
Men and women drinking wine
Young men drinking alomo
The beautiful day we can never forget

You grow like bamboo tree
Putting our eyes on you
As a firstlin
We knew you won't dissapoint us
You won many trophies
Who else don't know you
As an athlete of the field
But suddenly
Field as your burial ground
As you crept into the dark world

Now you put tears on our eye
A pity on us
We lost a great finger
Seeing your cadevre
Where you are laid
Now you are restless and speechless
You can't say I will!
Though,you are a great athlete
I saw all what you won
Tears once again,tears once again
The only bamboo tree
In the forest
Go to where people
